You will have a Discussion Test based on the Predict, Observe, Explain process that you have learned about in the course. Your instructor will put you into a group of 3 or 4 students. You will be shown a picture and a video of a product or piece of technology, and will need to answer questions about the picture and demonstrate your ability to predict, observe and explain during group discussion. The test will be approximately 20 minutes long. Refer to the rubric to see how you will be evaluated.
